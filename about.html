Who We Are

The Sane Africa Foundation, founded in 2021 by Chola Mumba (Robin), is a Lusaka-based NGO dedicated to addressing mental health challenges and empowering communities across Africa.

Mission

To promote mental health awareness, provide community-driven support, and empower individuals, especially children, women, and youth, to overcome mental health challenges and lead fulfilling lives.

Vision

A mentally healthy Africa where communities are compassionate, supportive, and equipped to help individuals thrive beyond mental health challenges.

Core Values

  • Compassion
  • Integrity
  • Inclusiveness
  • Empowerment
  • Collaboration
  • Innovation
